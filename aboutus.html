About Us

We're a small company that are tired of having to drag our phones everywhere to get information all the time. So instead we've created devices that alleviate that annoyance. Although our 'mirror display' isn't necessarily ground breaking technology, it has not been introduced to the consumer market and is only used by bigger companies like Nike, Target, etc. We want to bring this technology into the home of the consumer to create a personalised and affordable experience from the comfort and safety of your home.
